Nej Ravindran

As an artist, watercolor holds a special place in my heart. However, I embrace a wide range of mediums including acrylics, oils, and digital. With digital, I prefer to keep a traditional style, and my workflows follow are more aligned to traditional techniques than typical digital ones. My work is inspired by a wide variety of subjects: Landscapes, figurative pieces, portraits, and abstracts. Each creation reflects a moment of curiosity and discovery.

Though my primary profession lies in the world of technology, art has always been my way of being more expressive and creative. The knowledge of technology enhances my art rather than conflicting with it. For instance, it allowed me to build this website on my own without the need to outsource it to anyone else.

My goal as an artist is simple: to continually learn, grow, and push the boundaries of my creativity. I seek to evolve with each piece, experimenting with new techniques and ideas while honing my skills. In the future, I aspire to share this journey with a broader audience through exhibitions, hoping to connect with others who find beauty in the blending of art and technology, tradition and innovation. Ultimately, my art is a reflection of the joy I find in creating, and the endless possibilities that await exploration.

I invite viewers to join me in this journey of discovery, where every brushstroke and digital stroke leads to new insights and connections.
