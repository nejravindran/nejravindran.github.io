How to buy my artworks

Browse the gallery to see my works. All real-media arworks (watercolor, acrylic, oil) are done using high-quality professional-grade materials. Physical pieces not marked as "Sold" are available for purchase. Prints of all artworks are available even if they are marked as sold. Prints are done on stretched canvas (gallery wrap) on a wooden frame. Artworks can be purchased either directly or through my Etsy online shop. If you live in Peel or Toronto region of Canada, you can purchase directly so that you may be able to save on shipping. You can also purchse high-resolution digital copy of the paintings at a much cheaper price, which you can print at different sizes at your own convenience. You can also order custom artworks (eg. portrait of your loved ones, greeting cards for special occasions) in either digital or physical medium.


How to Order via Etsy

Visit my Etsy Shop (NRFineArtShop) and click "Request Custom Order" button. If the button is not visible, click envelope () icon as in the below image.

This will initiate a communication with me. An order listing will be created for you and sent to your Etsy email. Once you complete the payment, I will start working on the production and shipping/delivery. If your request is for a digital copy, the file will be emailed to you or delivered as a download link. You wlll be kept informed throughout the process via Etsy chat :-)
